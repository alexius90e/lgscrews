C3 Corrosion Resistance Screws For DIMPLE Holes Connections

Dimple holes are often used to create f stronger, more secure joint by allowing the screw to sit more firmly in the material. The indentation or ‘’dimple’ helps the screw or fastener to fit better, preventing slipping and providing a higher level of holding power.

Precision-Engineered Screws for Light Gauge Steel Framing

When it comes to light gauge steel framing, every connection counts. That’s why our screws are designed by expert engineers to deliver superior performance when fastening steel profiles through dimple holes.
Trilobular Fine Thread
Ensures optimal engagement and reduced strip-out for a secure, vibration-resistant hold.
Torx Slot
Provides excellent torque transfer, minimizing cam-out and enhancing installation efficiency.
Underhead Serrations
Improve grip and prevent loosening over time, ensuring long-lasting structural integrity.
C3 Corrosion Resistance
